Welcome

We are an electrical company aiming to provide affordable, reliable and competitive service to clients in and around the local Preston and wider North West area.

All work is carried out to the highest standard and complies with the current regulations. All our products comply with recognised British standards and have a 1 year guarantee.

We are experienced in all types of domestic and commercial electrical systems.

As an Electrical company we offer a full range of electrical services, from small jobs to full rewires.

We provide Landlords Electrical Certificates (known as EICR) and Testing to BS7671 and PAT testing. Landlords Certificates start from £180.00 depending on the size of the property.

We work for a number of builders, completing a range of small extensions to large house conversions and new build.

We have recently taken on electrical maintenance and installation work at 3 Blackpool Guest Houses. This includes on-call breakdown and planned maintenance work.

We pride ourselves on providing a fast and reliable service, and would be pleased to provide a quote for any forthcoming electrical projects you may have.
